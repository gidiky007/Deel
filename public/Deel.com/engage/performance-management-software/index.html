FAQs

What is employee performance software?

Employee performance software is a digital tool that helps organizations manage and enhance employee performance. It facilitates processes such as:

  • Setting performance goals
  • Conducting performance appraisals
  • Providing continuous feedback
  • Tracking progress.

The software aims to improve productivity, align individual performance with organizational goals, and support employee development through structured feedback and performance analytics.

Performance management software offers a broader suite of tools than performance tracking software. Apart from performance tracking, it includes goal setting, continuous feedback, performance appraisals, employee development plans, and integration with other HR processes.

What is performance tracking software?

Performance tracking software is a tool for monitoring and measuring employee performance against set goals and objectives. Its features include:

  • Real-time tracking of performance metrics
  • Automated reminders for deadlines
  • Analytics to assess progress and identify areas for improvement.

This software helps managers and employees stay aligned with performance expectations, facilitates timely feedback, and supports data-driven decision-making.

Performance tracking software is a component of performance management, which provides a more comprehensive framework for managing all aspects of employee performance and development.

What is a PMS system in HR?

A performance management system (PMS) in HR is a set of complementary processes organizations use to evaluate and improve employee performance. It includes:

  • Setting performance expectations
  • Monitoring progress
  • Providing ongoing feedback
  • Conducting performance reviews
  • Developing employee skills.

A PMS also helps align individual performance with organizational goals, enhances productivity, and supports career development.

What should I look for in an employee performance management solution?

Look for a software solution that allows you to:

  • Customize performance review cycles to your company's needs and goalsthis is possible with features such as anonymity settings, peer selection criteria, performance form builder, conditional questions, calibration, and customizable rating scales
  • Collect and share actionable, frequent, and meaningful insights from multiple sources (such as peers and supervisors)
  • Automate tedious tasks such as tracking goals, sending review forms, and sending reminders and notifications (via email or Slack) to ensure nobody misses steps or review cycles
  • Trigger performance evaluations automatically based on criteria like probation or start date
  • Reveal details of the larger picture of critical competencies, team performance, and leadership potential
  • Offer a user-friendly experience that admins, HR specialists, managers, and employees can easily access and navigate
  • Securely store and access company and employee data

What is the implementation process like, and how long does it typically take?

The implementation process for a performance review process can take up to 3 weeks, depending on various aspects, such as the existing performance system, the number of employees onboarded, and training needs.

The steps are:

  1. Meet stakeholders to assess the status quo, review requirements, and align on the desired "go live" date
  2. Design and create the performance review process
  3. Train managers and employees
  4. Onboard employees to the platform
  5. Launch the review
  6. Conduct additional manager training for interpreting and sharing results

Note that if you also need to include competencies in your review form, the process will take longer, as you will need to define competencies for all roles and levels within your organization.
