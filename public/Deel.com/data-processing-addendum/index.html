Data Processing Addendum

The Deel Data Processing Addendum (DPA) constitutes an integral part of the Terms of Service and is hereby incorporated into it by reference unless the Client/Contractor has signed a separate written DPA with Deel.

Deel's DPA forms part of the Main Agreement between Client and Deel covering the Client's use of the Services.

We provide pre-signed DPAs for our Clients and for Contractors.

  • Deel-Client DPA
  • Deel-Contractor DPA

Process for signing the DPA

  1. Download the pre-signed DPA.
  2. Add your information on page 1.
  3. Add your contact details relating to data transfers.
  4. Provide date and signature.
  5. Send back a signed and completed copy of the DPA to dpo@deel.com

Our DPA includes the new EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to align with the data protection safeguards in the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

If you have any questions about the DPA, feel free to contact our Privacy Compliance team at dpo@deel.com
