Overview

We are seeking an experienced, motivated individual to join our company as an Appointment Scheduler. As part of our team, you schedule appointments for clients, assist in managing company staff schedules and perform various data entry tasks necessary to the smooth functioning of the company. We operate with a high volume of clients and clients requests, meaning you must be well organized and able to multitask. Our ideal candidate has excellent phone and communication skills. 

Responsibilities:

  • Answer phones and direct calls to appropriate persons or service.

  • Assist clients in making new appointments or adjusting existing appointments.

  • Manage schedules for superior staffs.

  • Input new clients data and retrieve data for authorized requests.

Requirements:

  • High school diploma or GED certificate.

  • Prior administrative or customer service experience, preferably in a health care environment.

  • Strong communication skills, both written and verbal Data entry skills and experience.

  • Attention to detail and problem-solving ability.

Benefits:

  • 401k plan to participate in and save towards the future

  • 100% of medical, dental, and vision covered including 75% for dependents

  • Home office setup reimbursement

  • Monthly cell/internet reimbursement

  • Learning & Development / Education Reimbursement

  • Paternity / Maternity Leave

  • Birthday Benefit
