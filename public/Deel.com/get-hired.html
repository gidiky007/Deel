LOOKING FOR

Are you looking for workers?

If you’re an organization or recruiting firm looking to fill open roles across software engineering, data science, operations, or sales roles (to name a few), Project Get Hire/d was made for you.

With access to over 250k worker contracts, we’re connecting workers from Deel’s customer companies who are recently back on the job market with Deel’s recruiting firm partners to help place candidates in global, remote roles!

Step 1

Sign up and lock down some offer letters

With most of the workers in Deel’s network already operating as global, remote workers in fast-paced settings, we’ve got a workforce that’s ready to go! Connect with workers who are recently on the hiring market simply by opting in.

Get in touch with a global workforce  that’s ready to work

Add your information and answer a few questions. Once submitted, someone from our team will connect you with our partners so you can start filling roles.

Step 2

Ready, set, hire (globally)

Now that you’ve got access to top talent worldwide using some of our best recruiting partners, it’s time for you to find the perfect people to fill your open roles.

Still have questions?

Who is eligible to opt-in to this program?

While all of our hiring resources are available for anyone to use. The Project Get Hire/d program is only available to impacted EOR employees on Deel, not contractors.

Where are these resources from?

Deel’s Get Hired Hub is a collection of well-researched resources written by the Deel team to help get talent navigate the hiring process.

Can I share this with my network?

Of course! We’re dedicated to helping get impacted workers back on track and get companies the chance to pass on their great talent. So, please share the word.

Does Deel have any open roles?

We sure do! View our current roles and keep checking our Careers page for any new positions we add.
